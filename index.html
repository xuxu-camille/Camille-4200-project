Introduction

The Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) plays a critical role in determining the financial aid that millions of students receive annually. Analyzing FAFSA application trends provides valuable insights into socioeconomic patterns, accessibility to higher education, and institutional impacts. This study is important because it sheds light on how financial aid availability affects students' educational opportunities, particularly for underrepresented groups. It is also interesting as it explores the dynamic relationship between institutional policies and student financial behaviors, offering a comprehensive view of higher education financing.
